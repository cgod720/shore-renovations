DECK & RAILING

We provide a range of services to create a custom deck for your needs

Ready to revitalize your outdoor space? Shore Renovations is prepared to create, add on, and customize your dream deck. Our deck installation team will design and build your deck utilizing your chosen material, stain, and desired finish.

Specializing in complete deck installation, Shore Renovations also works on repairs and restorations. Summer at the shores of Monmouth and Ocean counties is further improved with safe and sturdy decking. Our experts are ready to help plan and design your deck today.

Our team provides full-service deck and railing remodeling along with the following services:

  • Installation of pressure-treated lumber or composite decking and railing
  • Deck Cleaning & Staining
  • Repair Stair Stringers & Baluster Replacement
  • Replacement of deck planks, posts, and stair treads
  • Composite Trex & Mahogany
  • Decking
  • Awning Installation
  • Garden Boxes

Prepare your outdoor space to be enjoyed all year long. Schedule a free estimate with a member of our design team today. Servicing Monmouth & Ocean Counties for over ten years, Shore Renovations is up to the task.
